About Yifeng Zhang

Yifeng Zhang is a New York-based family office principal focused on real estate acquisition across three defined verticals: raw land for residential and commercial development, Section 8 affordable housing, and land purchases in high-growth international markets. Operating through the Yifeng Zhang Family Office, he applies a long-term approach to identifying assets with cash flow stability, appreciation potential and strategic development value.

Yifeng Zhang Princem serves as the operational arm of the family office, supporting acquisition activity, development partnerships and hotel and hospitality brand collaborations. The structure allows the family office to evaluate domestic and international opportunities through a single acquisition framework while maintaining flexibility across asset types, geographies and deal timelines.

A closer look at how Yifeng Zhang structures real estate acquisition across the family office shows a globally oriented strategy built on discipline: Section 8 residential holdings and raw land acquisitions across domestic and international markets, each aligned with long-term value creation. Yifeng Zhang Princem supports the deal sourcing, acquisition execution and development partnerships that carry this strategy across every category the family office pursues.
